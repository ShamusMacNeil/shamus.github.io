Collecting and Reading Manga

Manga is basically the Japanese version of comic books. The major difference being in story telling, art-style, and the way you read each page.


I got into Manga collecting during the 2020 pandemic. I stumbled across some youtube videos of people with large collections, and thought how awesomed they looked.At the time I hadnt realized how much money one must spend in order to have an extravagant collection.
